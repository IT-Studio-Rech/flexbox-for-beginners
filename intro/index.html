Flexbox for beginners

Why flexbox?

  • The Flexbox layout is currently the most efficient way to to lay out, align and distribute space among items in a container, even when their size is unknown and/or dynamic (thus the word “flex”).
  • Easy possiblity to vertical-align (pain in the ass with css before the flexbox layout was established)
  • Super efficient way to reorder items
  • Orientation changing, resizing, stretching and shrinking made easy
